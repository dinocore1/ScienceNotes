# X-Ray Notes

## Interaction With Matter
There are 6 diffrent interactions the x-ray photon can have with matter. They are:

 * No Interaction
 * Rayleigh/Coherent Scattering
 * Compton Scattering
 * Photoelectric Absorption
 * Pair Production
 * Photodisintegration
